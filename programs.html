Past Events

Experience the impact of our past events that have transformed lives and strengthened our community.

Feb 23 2025

RE-IGNITE CONFERENCE

February 23rd 2025 was one to remember. RE-IGNITE CONFERENCE brought the heavens to Stream of Grace Chapel International as Bishop Dr Rain Onuigbo from Nigeria delivered a spirit filled Service. The Apostolic and Prophetic impartation conference saw people get healed, the man of God giving prophesies and God establishing deliverance. It was an awesome Sunday.

Nov 10 2024

Apostolic Impartation Conference

Apostolic Impartation Conference came off on the 10th of November 2024 and it was a spectacle to behold. The guest speaker on that day was Apostle Williams Nana Adjei from the United Kingdom. The event witnessed life changing teachings, powerful worship and the Impartation of the Holy Ghost. Overall it was a great experience and an encounter with God.
