Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our church and services.

What time are your Sunday services?

Our Sunday Bootcamp Service is held every Sunday from 8:30 AM to 10:30 AM. We encourage you to arrive about 15 minutes early to get settled and connect with others before the service begins.

Do you have programs for children?

Yes, we have a vibrant children's ministry that runs during our Sunday services. Our dedicated team provides age-appropriate teaching and activities to help children grow in their faith in a fun and engaging environment.

How can I become a member of the church?

We offer membership classes periodically throughout the year. These classes provide an overview of our church's vision, values, and beliefs. After completing the class, you'll have the opportunity to meet with one of our pastors and officially join our church family.

Do you offer counseling services?

Yes, our pastoral team provides spiritual counseling for various life situations. Whether you're facing challenges in your marriage, dealing with grief, or seeking guidance in other areas of life, our pastors are available to provide biblical counsel and prayer support.

How can I get involved in serving at the church?

We have numerous ministry opportunities available, from worship team to children's ministry, hospitality, media, and more. Fill out our volunteer form on this page or speak with one of our team members on Sunday to learn about current opportunities that match your gifts and interests.
